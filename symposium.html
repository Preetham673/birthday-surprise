Science Symposium 2025

About the Event

The Department of Science proudly presents the Annual Science Symposium 2025. This event brings together students, researchers, and professionals to share insights in emerging scientific fields.

  • Biotechnology & Healthcare Innovations
  • Environmental Science & Sustainability
  • Space Science & Astrophysics
  • Renewable Energy & Green Technologies

  • Prof. Rakesh Sharma – Biotechnology Researcher
  • Dr. Priya Menon – Environmental Scientist
  • Dr. Kiran Rao – Astrophysics Specialist
